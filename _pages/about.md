Selected Research Projects
======

**Multimodal Prediction of Response to Neoadjuvant Therapy in Rectal Cancer**  
*National First Prize – National Biomedical Engineering Innovation Competition*  

- Constructed a multimodal dataset integrating **MRI, colonoscopy images, and clinical variables**.  
- Performed medical image preprocessing and feature extraction.  
- Developed a **deep learning-based prediction model using PyTorch**.  
- Collaborated with clinicians to improve **clinical interpretability and translational potential**.

---

**Preoperative Assessment of Pancreatic Fibrosis Using Transfer Learning**

- Developed a **ResNet34-based transfer learning framework** for pancreatic CT classification.  
- Designed preprocessing pipelines including **bilinear interpolation and spatial normalization**.  
- Applied data augmentation strategies to mitigate **small dataset overfitting**.  
- Explored **layer-wise fine-tuning strategies** to improve model robustness.

---

**Multimodal Vision–Language Models for Medical Image Understanding**

- Investigated **vision–language models for medical image-text understanding**.  
- Conducted **few-shot learning experiments** to evaluate label efficiency.  
- Compared multiple baseline models to analyze **generalization under low-annotation settings**.
